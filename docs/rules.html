General Rules:


1. [Immutable] Friendship & Decipline are two different aspects & anyone should not mix between them.
2. [Immutable] Dedication & Discipline are the only qualities upon which the membership of a person should be decided.
3. There will be two sessions each week on MONDAY from 11:30AM & TUESDAY from 10:00AM.
4. Every member is assummed to check the group website everyday.
5. After a period of suspension if a MEMBER is unable to meet the TOTAL THRESOLD for a CONSECUTIVE 8 sessions, he will be suspendended for TWICE number of sessions he was suspended previously.
6. A suspension CONSECUTIVE of 20 sessions will lead to TERMINATION of his MEMBERSHIP
7. Every member should abide by the college regulatory rules.
8. Every Member should be a running student of the college.

Attendance:


9. A minimum 50% attendance is required. Attendance will be calculated after every EIGHT WORKING sessions.
10. If any MEMBER is found to have lower attendance than the prescribed thresold value, he will be suspended for corresponding 8 sessions starting from the day of counting attendance.

Presentation:


11. There will be atleast two topics per session.
12. The topics will be selected by voting.
13. Topics should be submitted online before 3 days of presentation.
14. Each presentation must be of minimum of 15 mins
15. After 5mins of presentation a topic can be discarded if found too stupid.
16. Mode of presentation will be strictly english. It will definitely increase the vocabulary & communication skill.
17. Every member should help the presenter if he is staggering to deliver the speech
18. The presenter during his presentation will have the power to throw any member outside the room for behaviour issues
19. No humiliation will be made to the presenter. If a member is found guilty of humiliating presenter, they will be suspended for that running session. `
20. A member MUST submit atleast 2 topics in every 8 working sessions.
21. If a member fails to submit atleast 2 topics within 8 working sessions then he will be suspended for 4 sessions.
22. If a member says that someone knows more with a disheartening sense then the speaker will be suspended for that session.

Voting:


23. Only those decisions will be implemented which will get 3/4th of the votes of the members
24. Voting will be done in both online & offline modes.
25. Critical voting will be done in person session in the room.

Founding Fathers


26. The founding fathers of the group are the heads of the group & any critical/administrative decision will be taken by the Founding Fathers only
27. [Immutable] NO Founding Father can be TERMINATED from the group but can be suspended for a FINITE time on basis of voting.
28. There will be three groups of Founding Fathers:
a) Disciplinary
b) Administrative
c) Organization
& a founding father will be appointed as the APEX who will be the APEX authority in the group
29. The powers of APEX authority is discussed in the Founding Fathers section
30. The APEX will be electected from the Founding Fathers & will be selected for 8 sessions only.
31. No Founding Father can serve as APEX for consecutive two terms & can serve only 3 terms in 100 sessions.

Admission


32. Admission of any individual will be done according to rule no 1
33. Any interested individual needs to fill up the admission form & will be granted as a general member after 8 working sessions as per the following conditions
a) He must maintain 75% attendance in 8 consecutive working sessions starting from the day of application
b) He must present atleast one topic in 8 consecutive working sessions starting from the day of application.
34. No individual can apply more than once in 100 working sessions.

Quiz


35. There will be a quiz session in 8 working sessions
36. The contents of the quiz will be decided by voting according to the voting rules
37. The format of quiz is discussed in the Quiz Rules

Ammendment


38. [Immutable] Any ammendment to the RULES will be decided by a critical voting session among the member according to voting rules.
39. [Immutable] Any voted ammendment as per previous clause to the RULES will be implemented after a seperate voting session among the Founding Fathers
40. [Immutable] This rule & any rule marked as Immutable can not be Amended.
