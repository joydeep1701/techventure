    What is Techventure

    A technical club where bunch of dedicated GCETTS students help each other to gain better understanding of technology

    Concepts

    We discuss the fundamental concepts of CS. Topics can be any thing from syllabus, with a greater scope of learning. We revise the concepts that are taught in classes. We help those who need more mentorship.

    Session Series

    Session Series is a dedicated series which are presented in long sessions. Here we present those topics which are beyond the scope of our current curriculum & can be helpful in future.

    Diversity of Content

    We have presentations ranging from fundamental digital electronics, architecture, assembly, C++, Data Structures & Algorithms, TOC to Quantum Computing, Cryptography & Machine Learning
